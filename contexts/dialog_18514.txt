CEDAR: Were you given an assignment to follow the activities of Longfellow Deeds?
BABE: Yes.
CEDAR: Did you subsequently write a series of articles about him?
BABE: Yes!
CEDAR: Are these the articles?
BABE: Yes!
CEDAR: Were you present when all these things took place?
BABE: Yes!
CEDAR: Are they true!
BABE: NO!!
CEDAR: But they did take place?
BABE: They're colored! Just to make him look silly!
CEDAR: And you saw them happen?
BABE: Yes, but I—
CEDAR: That's all, Miss Bennett.
BABE: It isn't all! I'd like to explain—
CEDAR: That's all, Miss Bennett. That's all.

MA STONE: My son ... what's the matter?
JABEZ: Ma! ... Where's Mary ... and little Dan'l?
MA STONE: Gone with Daniel Webster -- to Marshfield, son.... You told Mary to go--
JABEZ: Mr. Webster ... Wait! ... Mary!
MARY: Jabez!
JABEZ: Mary! Come back ...
MARY: Oh, Jabez! ... Did you hear, Mr. Webster? -- Now you'll help him, won't you?

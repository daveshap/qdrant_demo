SCRATCH: Excellent. A firm, fair signature. One that will last till doomsday. My dear Neighbor Stone, I congratulate you! You're going to be the richest man in New Hampshire!
JABEZ: Well, I'll be --
SCRATCH: Yes, indeed. But not now. Not for seven years. Oh, I almost forgot -- what is the date?
JABEZ: The seventh day of April --
SCRATCH: 1840. Well, that'll take us to the seventh day of April in 1847.

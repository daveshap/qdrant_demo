WEBSTER: Well, Martin, I only wear them in Washington -- that's the trouble. But if you ever come down there, I'll show them to you.
MARTIN: Gee, would you, Mr. Webster? Honest?
WEBSTER: Of course. And you tell your father for me -- we may be on opposite sides of the fence, but I'm always glad to hear of a man who holds to his own opinions. As long as people do that -- the country's all right. Do you understand, Martin?

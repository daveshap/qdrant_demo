JONES: What made you steal? Are you hungry?
BUCKLEY: No--I don't want to give any money. Everybody wants money--I'm not going to give any money.
JONES: Who wants your money-- maybe I can help you.
BUCKLEY: Maybe you want my money, too.
JONES: No, I don't want money. I only want to find out who you are and where you live. My business is finding peo- ple. Not taking their money.
BUCKLEY: You--a friend to me?
JONES: Why, yes, to you, Mr.--Mr. --what?
BUCKLEY: I don't know--I can't tell--

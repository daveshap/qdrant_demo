GENERAL: The enemy is in retreat. There will be no more fighting here. I came here to visit the crypts. My wife was buried here. What happened to the bodies?
ALBRECHT: They were gore before I came here.
GENERAL: But why?
ALBRECHT: There was some trouble here ——the villagers on the mainland —-this island was the focal point of their anger. They came here ——broke open the tombs and despoiled the graves.
GENERAL: All the graves?
ALBRECHT: I'm afraid so. There were rumors ——people were aroused. Some feared restlessness among the dead you know, the old superstitions.
GENERAL: I donut understand.

JEFFERSON: Is--is something the matter?
SAUNDERS: Oh, no--no! My dear *Senator*--it may be customary out on the prairie to take French leave of people and not be heard of again for five hours--
JEFFERSON: Gee--I'm sorry about that, Miss--you *are* Miss Saunders, aren't you?

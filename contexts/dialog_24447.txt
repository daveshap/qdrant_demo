HOOKS: But back in '34, Japanese-born could not <u>own</u> land. So...?
ETTA: Carl held it <u>for</u> 'em. Called it a lease. They make payments every June and December...
HOOKS: Why? If they could never take title.
ETTA: Their kids was born here. So when the oldest, that one there, was twenty...last payment gets made, and he could own it.

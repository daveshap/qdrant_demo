CATHY: Oh, no -— it's too delicious! You're making it up!
OLIVER: On my word! That's what they believe.
THEA: Please.. You shouldn't laugh --
OLIVER: You see? Thea believes it, too!
CATHY: Tell me about them, Thea. They have great wings end long teeth --
OLIVER: Sharp, shiny teeth -- and they creep up to your bed --
CATHY: Closer and closer —- until they bite into your throat!

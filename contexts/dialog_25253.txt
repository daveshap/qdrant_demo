THE BOY: Ojichan? Akira ojichaan dewa naino? Koko de nani shiteru no?
SULU: Gomen nasarei. Hito chigai de gozaranuka na. I'm sorry, my son. You have mis- taken me for someone else.
THE BOY: Ah, chigaau hito da. Hanashi kata ga okashii. Yes, this must be true. You talk funny.
SULU: Chotto omachi nasarei. Namae wa nanto moosareruka na. Wait my son. What is your name?
THE BOY: Sulu Hikaru.
SULU: Ah, sorenara mazu mazu nagaiki wo sareru to mira. Ah... Then I am sure that you will have a long and happy life.
THE BOY: Arigato. Sayonara. Thank you, honorable sir.

ERGO: Help! Help! I'm drowning!
COLWYN: I doubt it. The water is only an inch deep.
ERGO: It could have been quicksand! I might have been sucked to my death. Where is this place?
COLWYN: A forest near the Valley of Needles.
ERGO: Blast! A thousand miles off course. Well, I was rushed. There was a certain difference of opinion concerning a venison pie. The foolish man left it sitting on his windowsill. What did he expect?
COLWYN: Perhaps he expected to eat it.
ERGO: For that rudeness, peasant lout, I am going to leave you hanging by your heels when I depart. Which is right now.

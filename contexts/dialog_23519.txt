FENNYMAN: Mr. Shakespeare has given me the part of the Apothecary!
HENSLOWE: The Apothecary? Will, what is the story? Where is the shipwreck? How does the comedy end?
WILL: By God, I wish I knew.
HENSLOWE: By God, Will, if you do not, who does? Let us have pirates, clowns, and a happy ending, or we will send you back to Stratford to your wife!

IGOR: Your money may have been well invested, my friend. Something important may come of all this.
WORTH: Something important has got to come of it! Do you know that the rent on this place isn't paid?
IGOR: Is that a fact?
WORTH: No -- I'm lying to amuse myself! Now look here, I've an idea that will get us out of all this. We haven't twopence between us, but we've got these.
IGOR: Fire insurance!
WORTH: Yes, there's our way out. A fire in this place would give us ten thousand pounds.
IGOR: A fire! Is this your idea of humor, my friend?
WORTH: I want the money back I've thrown into this rubbish heap.

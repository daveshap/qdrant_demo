EMMA: What is it?
HUBERT: Sam Foley--dead!
EMMA: Great saints!
HUBERT: Of all the times! Of all the times! Two months to the end of his term-- and Foley has to go and die on us--
EMMA: Whom are you calling--in the dead of night?
HUBERT: Taylor, my dear.
EMMA: Can't that wait, Hubert?
HUBERT: No, no--believe me, pet--this is *most* urgent-- Hello, hello. Is Taylor there?-- Governor Hopper. Quickly, please--
EMMA: This isn't a home, it's the crossroads of the world!
HUBERT: Now, now, Emma, dear--you mustn't forget we have been chosen by the people of this commonwealth to--
EMMA: Save that for the laying of cornerstones, Hubert! Oh, that morning you looked in the mirror and saw a statesman!
HUBERT: Now, pet-- Jim!

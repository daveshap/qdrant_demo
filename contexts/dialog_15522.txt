TORQUIL: Passable, pimple, very passable.
ERGO: The greatest boon of your otherwise worthless life, blockhead, is the privilege of dining on boar roasted by the hand of Ergo the Magnificent.
TORQUIL: Your boast is a bigger mouthful than your roast, Magnificence.
TORQUIL: You've eaten nothing.
COLWYN: We must try to get horses.
TORQUIL: Yes. It will double our range. I know at least a dozen ways to get horses. All cheap.
COLWYN: These we'll pay for.
TORQUIL: Lad, you have an unnatural desire to pay for things. It stunts the mind and shrivels the imagination.
COLWYN: Hand over your dinner.
TORQUIL: A flicker of talent.

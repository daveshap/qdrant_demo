LOTHER: Can you read 'em Jack?
SUMMERS: They're a cinch to read--Queen of Clubs-- --Ace of Diamonds.
LOTHER: The man that marks them is an artist--two hundred and fifty bucks for six decks--and they're worth it.
SUMMERS: The country boy's about due.
LOTHER: Better unlock the door. Sit up, darling, and be dignified.

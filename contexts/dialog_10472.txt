WASHINGTON: Arnold is indispensable, do you understand? I need Arnold to help me win this war!
JOSEPH REED: Well, I shall deny him to you! He is evil on earth!
WASHINGTON: Are you all going insane!? Benedict Arnold! The man who stopped the British on Lake Champlain! Who carried the day at Saratoga! Goddamn-it, Joseph, you own two mansions, ride in an expensive, Tory coach! You are all nothing but a pack of greedy pigs!

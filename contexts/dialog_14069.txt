GENERAL: You do not know her last name -- you do not know from where she came?
ST. AUBYN: Miss Wollsten -— my secretary, she'd know.
GENERAL: Your daughter is ill.
ST. AUBYN: She's not too well.
GENERAL: What is her illness?
ST. AUBYN: Nothing, really. She's been under a great strain -- the journey -- the battle --
GENERAL: Was she ill before that girl came into your household?
ST. AUBYN: Why -- no not before Thea came ——
